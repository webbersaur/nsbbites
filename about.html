About NSB Bites

Your local guide to New Smyrna Beach's best restaurants and hidden gems

Our Story

NSB Bites was born from a love of New Smyrna Beach and its incredible food scene. Known as the "Shark Capital of the US," our beautiful coastal town offers so much more than just stunning beaches and world-class surfing - it's home to an amazing variety of restaurants, from waterfront seafood spots to cozy cafes on Flagler Avenue.

We created this guide to help locals and visitors alike discover the best places to eat in NSB. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner with ocean views, a family-friendly spot for Sunday brunch, or the perfect taco after a day at the beach, we've got you covered.

What We Do

NSB Bites is your comprehensive resource for dining in New Smyrna Beach. We personally visit and review local restaurants, highlighting their unique features, cuisine types, and what makes each spot special. Our goal is to connect hungry visitors and residents with the perfect dining experience.

We also offer exclusive NSB Bites merchandise so you can rep your favorite beach town wherever you go. From t-shirts to hoodies, our gear celebrates the unique spirit of New Smyrna Beach.
